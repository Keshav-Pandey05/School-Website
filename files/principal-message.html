Home › Signatories › Principal's Message

Mrs. Sunita Sharma

Principal, Subhash Public School

  • M.A. in English Literature, DU
  • B.Ed., Delhi University
  • CTET Qualified
  • 28+ Years Teaching Experience
  • State Best Teacher Award, 2015
  • Child Safeguarding Certified
"The greatest gift we can give our children is not just knowledge, but the confidence to use it and the character to use it wisely."

Dear Parents, Students, and the Subhash Public School Family,

It is a privilege beyond words to serve as the Principal of Subhash Public School. Each morning when I walk through our gates and see young faces full of wonder and energy, I am reminded of why we chose education as our life's work — and I am grateful anew.

Over the past two-and-a-half decades, I have had the honour of guiding thousands of students through their most formative years. I have watched shy seven-year-olds blossom into confident young adults who go on to become doctors, engineers, artists, entrepreneurs, and above all, good human beings. There is no reward greater than this.

At Subhash Public School, we believe that a school is not just a building — it is a community. A community built on mutual respect, shared purpose, and the belief that every single child matters. Our teachers are carefully selected not just for their subject expertise but for their empathy, their patience, and their passion for teaching. They form the heart of everything we do here.

Academically, we are proud of our consistent record: a 98% CBSE pass rate, top district rankers year after year, and students who go on to secure admissions to the country's finest colleges and universities. But we measure our success in far more than examination results. We celebrate the student who overcame stage fright to perform in the school play, the child who learned to swim after months of effort, the quiet reader who finally found their voice through creative writing.

We have worked hard to build an environment where learning is joyful and challenges are embraced rather than feared. Our co-curricular programmes — from robotics clubs and debate teams to classical dance and community service projects — ensure that students develop skills and interests that will serve them throughout life.

To our students: you are the reason we are here. Come to school curious. Ask questions you think might sound foolish — because the best questions often do. Support each other. Be kind to the child who is finding things difficult. Step up when leadership is needed. The habits you build here will define who you become.

To our parents: you are our most important partners. Keep talking to your children about school — not just about marks, but about friendships, about what excited them today, about what worried them. Please reach out to us whenever you have a concern. An open conversation is always better than a growing worry.

Together — school and home, teachers and parents, students and staff — we form an unbeatable team. And together, there is nothing our children cannot achieve.

With warm regards and every good wish for the year ahead,

Mrs. Sunita Sharma

Principal, Subhash Public School
