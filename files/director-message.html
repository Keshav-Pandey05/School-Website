Mr. Keshav Pandey

Director, Subhash Public School

  • Ph.D. in Educational Leadership, Delhi University
  • M.Ed., Jamia Millia Islamia
  • 35+ Years in Education
  • Former CBSE Board Examiner
  • National Teacher Awardee, 2008
  • Author of 3 Educational Books
"Education is not the filling of a pail, but the lighting of a fire. At Subhash Public School, we have always believed that every child carries within them an extraordinary spark waiting to be ignited."

Dear Students, Parents, and Well-Wishers,

It is with immense pride and deep humility that I welcome you to Subhash Public School — an institution that has, for nearly four decades, stood as a beacon of quality education, ethical values, and holistic development in our community.

When we established Subhash Public School in 1985, our dream was simple yet profound: to create a school where every child — regardless of background or ability — would be given the tools, the environment, and the encouragement to discover their best self. Looking at where we stand today, with over 3,200 students, 180 dedicated faculty members, and thousands of successful alumni spanning the globe, I believe we have honoured that promise.

Education today stands at a fascinating crossroads. The world our children will inhabit as adults is one we can barely imagine — shaped by artificial intelligence, climate change, global interconnectedness, and challenges we have not yet encountered. Our responsibility as educators is not merely to transfer knowledge, but to cultivate the capacities that will allow our students to navigate an uncertain future with confidence, creativity, and compassion.

At Subhash Public School, we have invested deeply in a curriculum that balances academic rigour with experiential learning. Our teachers are not lecturers but mentors — individuals who know each child by name, who notice when a student is struggling, and who go the extra mile to ensure no one is left behind. Our infrastructure, from the advanced science laboratories to the performing arts centre, exists to serve one purpose: providing every child with the richest possible learning experience.

To our students, I say: embrace your curiosity. Ask questions. Make mistakes and learn from them. Collaborate generously and compete with integrity. The world needs problem-solvers, innovators, and kind-hearted human beings — and that is precisely who Subhash Public School is here to help you become.

To our parents and guardians, I extend my deepest gratitude. Your trust in us is the greatest honour we receive each day. We pledge to partner with you closely, to keep communication open, and to always put the well-being of your children at the centre of every decision we make.

I look forward to a future rich with achievement, discovery, and community — one brilliant young mind at a time.

Dr. Ramesh Kapoor

Director, Subhash Public School
