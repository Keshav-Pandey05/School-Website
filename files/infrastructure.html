15-Acre Campus

Built for Brilliance

Our campus combines modern architecture with thoughtfully designed learning spaces that inspire curiosity, collaboration, and creativity at every turn.

🖥️

Smart Classrooms

All 80 classrooms are equipped with interactive smart boards, high-speed Wi-Fi, and multimedia projection systems. Air-conditioned environments ensure optimal learning comfort throughout the year.

80 Classrooms Smart Boards AC + Wi-Fi
🔬

Advanced Science Labs

Separate, fully-equipped labs for Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Environmental Science. Each lab accommodates 30 students simultaneously with individual workstations and safety equipment.

4 Labs Individual Workstations Latest Instruments
💻

Digital Learning Centre

Three state-of-the-art computer labs with 250+ workstations running the latest software. High-speed fibre internet, robotics kits, 3D printers, and AI learning tools make this a truly future-ready space.

250+ Computers 3D Printers Robotics Kits
📚

Resource Library

A 5,000 sq. ft. two-storey library housing over 40,000 titles across all genres and subjects. Features digital cataloguing, e-library access, reading pods, and a dedicated research room for senior students.

40,000+ Books E-Library Reading Pods
⚽

Sports Complex

Our world-class sports facilities include a full-size football ground, cricket pitch with practice nets, four basketball courts, two badminton halls, a 400m athletics track, and a well-equipped fitness centre.

Football Ground Cricket Pitch Athletics Track
🏊

Olympic Swimming Pool

A 50-metre, 8-lane Olympic-standard swimming pool with heated water, underwater lighting, and a dedicated learner's pool for beginners. Coached by national-level swimmers and certified safety staff.

50-metre Pool 8 Lanes Heated Water
🎭

Performing Arts Centre

A 600-seat air-conditioned auditorium with professional lighting, acoustics, and backstage facilities. Separate studios for music (with 40 instruments), classical and contemporary dance, and theatre/drama.

600-Seat Auditorium Music Studio Dance Studios
🍽️

Modern Cafeteria

A hygienic, spacious dining hall serving nutritionally balanced vegetarian meals prepared fresh every day. Menus are designed by a certified nutritionist and rotate weekly to provide variety and proper nourishment.

500+ Capacity Nutritionist-Designed 100% Hygienic
🏥

Health & Wellness Centre

A fully equipped on-campus health centre staffed by a qualified doctor and two nurses during school hours. Separate counselling rooms, a dental check-up bay, and a quiet rest area for unwell students.

Full-Time Doctor Counselling Rooms Emergency Care
