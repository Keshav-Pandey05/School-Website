Our Story

38 Years of Shaping Futures

Established in 1985, Subhash Public School was founded on a singular vision — to create an institution where every child could discover their unique potential. What began as a modest school with 120 students has grown into one of the region's most respected educational institutions, serving over 3,200 students today.

We are proudly affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) and hold an A+ grade — a testament to our unwavering commitment to academic standards, teacher quality, and student welfare.

Our 15-acre campus in the heart of New Delhi houses world-class facilities that provide students with a learning environment second to none. From advanced science laboratories and a fully digital library to an Olympic-size swimming pool, we invest in every dimension of a child's development.

Vision, Mission & Values

🔭

Our Vision

To be a globally recognised centre of excellence that empowers every student to become a lifelong learner, ethical leader, and compassionate global citizen — ready to make a meaningful difference in the world.

🎯

Our Mission

To provide a stimulating, inclusive, and values-driven learning environment where academic rigour meets creative exploration, and where every child is seen, heard, and inspired to reach their highest potential.

⚖️

Core Values

Integrity · Excellence · Empathy · Innovation · Responsibility. These five pillars underpin everything we do — from how we teach to how we treat one another — creating a school culture of trust and growth.

🌱

Our Approach

We follow a child-centric pedagogy that balances structured learning with enquiry-based discovery. Small class sizes, mentorship, and a holistic co-curricular programme ensure no student is left behind.
