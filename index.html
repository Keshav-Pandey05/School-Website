Who We Are

A Legacy of Learning & Character

Founded in 1985, Subhash Public School has been a beacon of quality education in the region. We are affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) and pride ourselves on a holistic approach that goes far beyond textbooks.

Our sprawling 15-acre campus is home to state-of-the-art labs, a well-stocked library, performing arts facilities, and Olympic-standard sports grounds — everything a young learner needs to thrive.

    Academic Excellence through personalised learning
  • 🌱
    Character Building rooted in ethics and empathy
  • 🌍
    Global Mindset with local values
  • 🏆

What Makes Us Different

We go beyond academics to nurture well-rounded individuals ready for the world.

Advanced Science Labs

Fully equipped physics, chemistry, and biology laboratories with latest instruments that bring science to life for every student.

💻

Digital Learning Hub

Smart classrooms, high-speed internet, and computer labs running industry-standard software to prepare students for the digital era.

🎨

Creative Arts Centre

Music, dance, theatre, visual arts — our dedicated performing arts block nurtures every student's creative spark.
